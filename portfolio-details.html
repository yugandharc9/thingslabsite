Project Case Studies

A deeper look at how our products solve real problems.

AlertPulse

IoT Emergency Alert System
IoT Real-Time Cloud MQTT Mobile Alerts

Deployed for environments requiring round-the-clock hazard monitoring — from industrial facilities to residential buildings.

View Product
Problem

Traditional smoke and temperature alarms are passive — they sound locally but offer no remote awareness, automated response, or data logging. Operators had no way to know about incidents unless physically present.

Solution
Internet-Connected Sensors — devices push events to the cloud the moment a trigger is detected.
Multi-Channel Alerts — SMS, email, and mobile push notifications ensure someone always gets notified.
Automated Actions — the system can cut power, trigger sirens, or lock doors without human intervention.
Cloud Dashboard — full event history, device health, and configuration from any browser.

SmartSwitchGuard

IoT Emergency Notifier Switch System
IoT Remote Control Energy Safety Automation

Built for homes, offices, and industrial setups that need remote device control with built-in safety failsafes.

View Product
Problem

Appliances left on accidentally — or worse, during a fire or flood — cause preventable damage. Existing smart plugs lacked emergency integration, energy monitoring, and grouping for complex setups.

Solution
Remote On/Off — control any connected appliance from anywhere via app or web interface.
Emergency Auto-Shutoff — sensor triggers automatically cut power before damage escalates.
Energy Monitoring — per-device consumption tracking with overage alerts.
Smart Modes — Vacation Mode and Away Mode auto-manage devices based on occupancy rules.
