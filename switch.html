SmartSwitchGuard

Your Ultimate IoT Emergency Notifier Switch System

System Concept

SmartSwitchGuard is an Internet-connected switch system designed to provide remote control, emergency alerts, and automatic safety responses for connected devices. Whether at home, in the office, or on the go, you can ensure the safety of your environment and appliances.

Key Features

Remote Control of Devices

Control lights, fans, security systems, and other appliances remotely from anywhere through an app or web interface.

Real-Time Emergency Alerts

Receive instant notifications via email, SMS, or mobile app when smoke, temperature, or motion sensors detect a potential emergency.

Automatic Emergency Response

SmartSwitchGuard can automatically turn off appliances during emergencies to prevent further damage and ensure safety.

User-Friendly Dashboard & Alerts

The intuitive dashboard lets you track device status, receive live notifications, and manage connected devices with ease. Emergency alerts are pushed instantly to your mobile app or via SMS.

Customizable Emergency Triggers

Set up temperature, smoke, or motion alerts with automatic actions, such as turning off appliances or activating security measures, to protect your home or office.

Energy Consumption Monitoring

Monitor energy usage for each device connected to SmartSwitchGuard and get alerts when power consumption exceeds predefined limits.

Safety Modes for Extra Protection

Activate Vacation Mode or Away Mode to ensure all devices are turned off when no one is around, or set up emergency overrides for critical devices like medical equipment.

Cloud-Based Integration

SmartSwitchGuard syncs data to the cloud for real-time control and access, even during network or power failures. Keep your devices connected and synced no matter where you are.

Multi-Device Support

Control multiple devices simultaneously or group them together. Manage entire rooms or buildings with a single action.
