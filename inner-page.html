Who We Are

Thingslab Internet Services is a technology company based in India, specialising in IoT product development, web engineering, and data solutions. We build real, useful technology — from embedded hardware systems to cloud-connected dashboards — for businesses and communities that need reliable digital tools.

Founded with a passion for smart systems and purposeful innovation, our small but focused team ships products that work in the real world, not just on paper.
